Can you do an ice bath without a machine?

Yes. You can fill a sturdy tub with cold tap water and add bags of ice or frozen water bottles to reach the desired temperature.

Tips for chilling the water

Start with cold tap water, then gradually add ice. Use a thermometer to monitor temperature, aiming for 50–59°F (10–15°C) for a moderate plunge.

Maintaining the cold

To keep the water cold longer, choose a well-insulated tub and place it in a shaded area. Add additional ice as needed during your session.

FAQ

Wondering how much ice to use or how long to stay in? Begin with shorter sessions and adjust based on comfort, adding ice gradually.
